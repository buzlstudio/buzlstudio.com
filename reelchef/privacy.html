Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Buzl Studio ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use ReelChef. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and display name for account creation
  • Content you create and save within the app
  • Subscription status and usage information
  • Device token for push notifications (with your permission)
  • Language preference

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our service
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To send push notifications and transactional emails

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for core app functionality including authentication, data storage, content processing, and payment management. No personal data is shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely with encrypted transmission (TLS). We implement access controls to ensure users can only access their own data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, as well as the right to object to or restrict processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@buzlstudio.com.

7. Children's Privacy

ReelChef is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services. We do not collect behavioral data or share your data with advertisers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of ReelChef after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
