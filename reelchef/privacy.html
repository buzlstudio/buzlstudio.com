Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Buzl Studio ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use ReelChef. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Preferred language setting

Usage Data:

  • Instagram Reel URLs you submit for processing
  • Recipes generated and saved in your account
  • Shopping lists and collections you create
  • Subscription status and usage counts

Device Information:

  • Device token for push notifications (only with your permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the ReelChef service
  • To process recipes from submitted Instagram Reel URLs
  • To send push notifications about recipe processing status
  • To manage your subscription and enforce usage limits
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset)

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for authentication, data storage, AI processing, push notifications, and payment management. These services are selected for their strong security and privacy practices. No personal data is shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. All data transmission is encrypted using TLS. We implement row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data (recipes, shopping lists, collections) is permanently deleted.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification - Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability - Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection - Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction - Request restriction of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@buzlstudio.com.

7. Children's Privacy

ReelChef is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Push Notifications

We send push notifications to inform you when recipe processing is complete. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

9. No Tracking or Analytics

ReelChef does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. We do not collect behavioral data, create user profiles for advertising, or share your data with advertisers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of ReelChef after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
